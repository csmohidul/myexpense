Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. As you use the service, we also store the expense records you enter — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions — along with account activity needed to keep the service running smoothly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve Spendly's core functionality: authenticating your account, displaying your expense history, and generating summaries and category breakdowns. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time, after which your records will be permanently removed from our systems within a reasonable period.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of third-party services to operate, such as font and hosting providers, which may process limited technical data (like IP address) as part of delivering the site. We do not share your expense data with any third party for their own marketing or analytics purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on your account page, and we'll be happy to help.
