Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly collects, how we use it, and the choices you have as a user of our personal expense tracking application ("Service").

Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. As you use the Service, we also store the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Service — for example, to display your expense history, generate spending summaries, and authenticate your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and are only accessible to you through your authenticated account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal expense data with third parties. We may rely on trusted infrastructure providers to host the Service, but any such providers are bound to handle data only as necessary to operate Spendly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app.
